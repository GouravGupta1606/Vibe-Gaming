🧠 Memory Match

Test your focus and short-term memory in this card-flipping challenge. Flip two cards — if they match, they stay open. Match all 8 pairs in the fewest moves to win!

How to Play

  • Click any card to flip it and reveal the emoji.
  • Click a second card — if they match, they stay face-up.
  • Continue until all 8 pairs are matched.
  • Card positions randomize every game.

🏃 Endless Runner

Sprint through a neon-lit world dodging relentless obstacles. Jump, double-jump, and duck — the longer you survive, the faster it gets!

How to Play

  • SPACE or ↑ to jump.

🎮 Tic Tac Toe

Classic 1v1 offline on the same device. Player 1 is ✖ and Player 2 is ⭕. Get three in a row to win — or it's a draw!

How to Play

  • Player 1 = ✖  ·  Player 2 = ⭕. X always starts.
  • Click any empty square to place your symbol.
  • Three in a row (row, column, or diagonal) wins.
  • Hit 🔄 Reset to play again anytime.

🐍 Snake Game

The timeless classic! Control the hungry snake, collect food to grow longer, but avoid the walls and your own tail. How long can you survive?

How to Play

  • Arrow keys ↑ ↓ ← → to move the snake.
  • Eat food icons to grow and score points.
  • Avoid walls and your own tail — game over if you hit them.
  • The snake speeds up as you score — stay sharp!

🧱 Brick Breaker

Upgraded neon brick breaker with 8 levels, power-ups, lives, combo multiplier, particle explosions, and mouse control. Destroy all bricks to advance!

How to Play

  • Move paddle with ← → arrow keys or mouse.
  • SPACE to launch ball. P to pause.
  • Collect glowing power-ups: Wide, Slow, Fireball, +Life.
  • Chain hits for a combo multiplier bonus score.
  • 3 lives — don't let the ball fall! 8 levels total.
